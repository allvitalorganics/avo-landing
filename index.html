Strategy전략 · 왜 단일 페이지인지

Read this once · the why behind the structure · locked

Cold visitors arrive via IG (after a Reel lands), Naver search ("AVO 샐러두유"), word-of-mouth referral, or press pickup. They don't know the brand yet. They have ~8 seconds to decide if this is worth their attention. The landing page has one job: convert that attention into a Naver or Coupang click.

Three principles

PrincipleWhy
One scroll, full story.Korean PDPs are long (기승전결 convention). Visitors expect to scroll. Don't make them click between pages to learn what we are.
Shop is off-site.Korean consumers trust Naver + Coupang more than DTC checkout. The Shopify site is a brand legitimization touchpoint, not a checkout. CTAs route to Naver / Coupang.
Witness voice, not teacher.The site mirrors the IG voice. First-person observation. Specific details. No "you should." No medical claims. 일반식품 lane only.

Single page now → multi-page later

PhaseSite structure
Day 1 → Month 6 (current)Single page. / = brand story + product + buy CTAs.
Month 6+ (SKU 2 launches)Split: / = brand homepage, /products/salad-soymilk, /products/phyto-complete, /about, /story/* (essays/episodes).
Year 2+Full Shopify storefront if and only if Korean DTC checkout becomes a real channel (after marketplace primacy validated). Until then Shopify = brand site.
Decision rule: the Shopify site exists to make AVO feel real and answer "wait, who is this brand?" The first 10K buyers will come through Naver/Coupang funnels — they Googled, they landed here, they trusted what they saw, they clicked back to buy where they're used to buying.

Q. How is this different from a supplement?
A. It's not a supplement. It's food. We don't promise drug-like outcomes. It's real food concentrated into one serving.

Q. 임산부도 마셔도 되나요?
A. 일반식품이며 의약품이 아닙니다. 임산부는 개별 영양 상담 후 결정 권장.
Q. Can I drink this during pregnancy?
A. This is general food, not medicine. Consult a nutritionist or doctor before incorporating.

A. 대두·밀 알레르기 가능 원료 포함. 알레르기 확인 후 결정 권장.
Q. Is it safe for kids?
A. Contains soy and wheat-allergen-adjacent ingredients. Confirm allergies before giving.

A. One. With 5g fiber per pouch, more than two might be heavy on digestion.

A. 식물·콩 베이스의 자연식 맛. 일반 두유보다 진하고, 코코넛꽃액즙으로 자연 단맛.

A. Plant + soy natural base. Thicker than regular soy milk. Natural sweetness from coconut flower nectar.

A. Cool and dry, out of direct sunlight. Drink immediately once opened. Shelf-stable at room temperature (sterilized product).
